The Four Temperaments

Temperaments refers to a person's characteristics patterns of behavior. emotions, and reactions, which are believed to be biologically based and relatively stable over time. It is often used to describe individual differences in personality traits.

There are several different theories and models of temperament, but we will provide a brief overview of the most well-known ones:

Choleric

ambitious, energetic, easily angered

Phlegmatic

calm, easygoing, unemotional

Melancholic

thoughtful, introverted, prone to sadness

Sanguine

optimistic, social, outgoing

HOW DO I SCHEDULE AN APPOINT FOR AN IN-PERSON CONSULTATION

To schedule an appointment for an in-person consultation, you can call our office during business hours or use our online booking system on our website. Choose a suitable date and time, and our receptionist will confirm the appointment with you via email or phone.

IS IT POSSIBLE TO ACCESS CONSULTATIONS ONLINE

Yes, we offer online consultations for your convenience. Our platform allows you to schedule appointments with our qualified professionals through secure virtual channels. You can discuss your concerns, receive expert advice, and get the assistance you need from the comfort of your own home. Feel free to reach out to us to book an online consultation and address your specific needs efficiently.

WHAT CAN I EXPECT DURING MY FIRST THERAPY SESSION

During your initial therapy session, your psychologist will conduct an intake assessment to gather relevant information about your background, concerns, and goals for therapy. This information helps them better understand your unique situation and develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.

HOW LONG DOES EACH THERAPY SESSION TYPICALLY LAST

Each therapy session usually lasts around 45 to 60 minutes. However, the duration may vary based on individual requirements and the type of therapy being provided. Your psychologist will discuss the session length with you and adjust it if necessary to ensure the most effective treatment.

WHAT IF I NEED TO RESCHEDULE OR CANCEL AN APPOINTMENT

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise, making it necessary to reschedule or cancel an appointment. If you need to make changes, please inform us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to accommodate other clients in need of our services. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee.
